The Aurum Bespoke Journey

📋
1

Consultation & Design

Our master tailor visits your home or office at your convenience to understand your style preferences, lifestyle needs, and specific requirements. We discuss fabric options from our curated collection of premium materials, design elements, and create a personalized plan for your garment with detailed sketches.

  • ✓ In-depth style consultation: We understand your personal style, occasions, and preferences
  • ✓ Fabric selection guidance: Access to premium fabrics from Italian and British mills
  • ✓ Design customization options: Personalize every detail from lapel style to button choice
  • ✓ Detailed sketches: Visual representation of your custom design before creation
📐
2

Precision Measurement

Using traditional tailoring techniques combined with modern precision tools, we take over 20 detailed measurements to ensure a perfect fit. This meticulous process accounts for your body's unique proportions and posture.

  • ✓ Over 20 detailed measurements: Including shoulder slope, arm length, and torso proportions
  • ✓ Posture and movement analysis: Understanding how you sit, stand, and move
  • ✓ Digital measurement recording: Ensuring consistency and accuracy for future garments
  • ✓ Fit profile creation: Your unique measurements stored for future reference
🧵
3

Craftsmanship & Creation

Our master tailors begin the meticulous crafting process, combining time-honored techniques with modern precision. Each garment undergoes multiple quality checks during the creation process to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

  • ✓ Handcrafted construction: Each stitch placed with precision by experienced artisans
  • ✓ Multiple quality checkpoints: Rigorous inspection at every stage of creation
  • ✓ Premium fabric handling: Specialized techniques for different fabric types
  • ✓ Attention to detail: Finishing touches that make the difference
✅
4

Fitting & Delivery

We schedule a final fitting at your convenience to ensure perfection. Any necessary adjustments are made before the garment is carefully packaged and delivered to you. We also provide care instructions to maintain the garment's pristine condition.

  • ✓ Final fitting session: Ensure the perfect fit and make any final adjustments
  • ✓ Final adjustments if needed: Minor tweaks for absolute perfection
  • ✓ Care instructions provided: Guidance on maintaining your garment's quality
  • ✓ Premium packaging: Your garment delivered in protective luxury packaging
