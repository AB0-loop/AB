The Aurum Legacy

Founded in 2018 by master tailor Rajesh Kumar, Aurum Bespoke emerged from a desire to bring world-class tailoring directly to the homes of Bangalore's discerning gentlemen. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a team of skilled artisans dedicated to the art of bespoke menswear.

Our journey began when Rajesh noticed that many professionals in Bangalore struggled to find well-fitting, high-quality clothing that suited their lifestyle. With over 20 years of experience in traditional tailoring and a passion for innovation, he set out to create a service that combined the convenience of home visits with the precision of atelier-quality craftsmanship.
