Common Questions

How long does the entire process take? ▼

Our standard process takes approximately 3-4 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery. This includes:

  • • Initial consultation and design selection (1-2 days)
  • • Precision measurements and pattern creation (1-2 days)
  • • Crafting and quality control (2-3 weeks)
  • • Final fitting and delivery (1-2 days)

For urgent requirements, we offer express service with a 2-week turnaround for an additional fee.

These prices include premium fabrics, handcrafted construction, and home visit consultations. Custom fabric options are available at additional cost.

Do you provide fabric options? ▼

Yes, we offer an extensive selection of premium fabrics from renowned mills including:

  • • Italian fabrics from Ermenegildo Zegna, Loro Piana, and Carlo Barbera
  • • British fabrics from Scabal, Holland & Sherry, and Dormeuil
  • • Indian luxury fabrics including handwoven silks and premium cottons
  • • Seasonal collections featuring the latest textures and patterns

During your consultation, our design experts will guide you through fabric options that best suit your style, occasion, and budget.
