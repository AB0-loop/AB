Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Everything you need to know about our bespoke tailoring services

What types of garments do you create? ▼

We specialize in bespoke menswear including suits, blazers, shirts, sherwanis, bandgalas, pathani suits, modi jackets, and indo-western fusion wear. Each garment is custom-made to your measurements and preferences.

Do you offer women's wear or children's clothing? ▼

Currently, we specialize exclusively in men's bespoke clothing. We focus on perfecting our craft for gentlemen's wear to ensure the highest quality and attention to detail.

What is the price range for your garments? ▼

Our pricing varies based on garment type and fabric selection:

  • Tailored Shirts: ₹3,500 - ₹8,000
  • Blazers: ₹12,000 - ₹25,000
  • Bespoke Suits: ₹25,000 - ₹60,000
  • Sherwanis: ₹18,000 - ₹45,000
  • Bandgalas: ₹15,000 - ₹35,000

Premium fabrics and complex designs may increase the price. We offer a range of options to suit different budgets while maintaining our quality standards.

Do you offer payment plans or financing options? ▼

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including:

  • 50% deposit at the time of booking, balance on delivery
  • EMI options through our payment partners
  • Special financing for wedding collections

Contact us to discuss payment options that work for your budget.

How long does the entire process take? ▼

Our standard delivery time is 3 weeks from the initial consultation:

  • Day 1-2: Initial consultation and design selection
  • Day 3-5: Precision measurements and pattern creation
  • Week 1-3: Crafting and quality control process
  • Week 3-4: Final fitting and garment delivery

For urgent requirements, we offer express service (2 weeks) with a nominal additional charge.

How does the home visit consultation work? ▼

Our home visit service is designed for your convenience:

  • Schedule a visit at your preferred time (morning, afternoon, or evening)
  • Our master tailor brings fabric samples and design books to your location
  • Consultation typically takes 45-60 minutes
  • We cover Bangalore metro area with no additional travel charges
  • For locations beyond our standard coverage, nominal travel fees may apply

Book your consultation through WhatsApp, phone, or our website contact form.

What fabric options do you offer? ▼

We offer a curated selection of premium fabrics sourced from renowned mills:

  • Italian Fabrics: Ermenegildo Zegna, Loro Piana, Carlo Barbera
  • British Fabrics: Scabal, Holland & Sherry, Willsink & Co
  • Indian Fabrics: Premium khadi, handwoven silks, and cottons
  • Specialty Fabrics: Linen blends, velvet, and seasonal collections

During your consultation, our tailor will guide you through fabric options based on garment type, season, and your preferences.

Can I provide my own fabric? ▼

Yes, we welcome client-provided fabrics. However, please note:

  • Fabric must be of sufficient quantity for the garment type
  • We assess fabric quality to ensure it meets our standards
  • Some complex patterns may require additional planning
  • Client-provided fabric garments may have different warranty terms

Discuss your fabric with our tailor during the consultation to ensure it's suitable for your chosen garment.

What if my garment needs alterations after delivery? ▼

We stand behind our craftsmanship with our alteration guarantee:

  • Free alterations within 30 days of delivery for any fit issues
  • Lifetime complimentary alterations for minor adjustments
  • Express alteration service available within 48 hours
  • Home visit for alterations in Bangalore metro area

Contact us immediately if you notice any issues with your garment. We're committed to ensuring perfect fit and satisfaction.

How should I care for my bespoke garments? ▼

Proper care ensures your garments last for years:

  • Shirts: Machine wash cold, tumble dry low or line dry
  • Suits/Blazers: Dry clean only, use padded hangers
  • Sherwanis/Bandgalas: Professional cleaning, store in garment bags
  • Rotate garments to prevent wear patterns
  • Address stains immediately to prevent setting

We provide detailed care instructions with each garment and offer lifetime care guidance.
